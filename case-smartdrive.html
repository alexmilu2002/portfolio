Plate I

Smart DriveUX/UI for a data platform.

Duration3 weeks TypeUX/UI design ToolsAdobe XD
Responsive interface across desktop and mobile. Fig. 01

SmartDrive is a UX/UI design project focused on creating a responsive digital platform that provides users with clear and accessible vehicle information. The objective of this assignment was to design a digital product that could display a large amount of data in a structured and user-friendly way while maintaining a consistent experience across multiple devices, including desktop, tablet, and mobile.

One of the key requirements of the project was to create a fully responsive interface that adapted seamlessly to different screen sizes without compromising usability or readability. I had complete creative freedom in determining both the target audience and the purpose of the product, allowing me to develop a concept that addressed a real-world problem. Before starting the design process, I created a detailed user scenario that defined the needs, goals, and expectations of the intended users. This scenario became the foundation for every design decision throughout the project.

The concept I developed, called AutoInsight, is a smart data platform that collects, organizes, and analyzes vehicle information for multiple user groups, including car dealerships, garages, manufacturers, and private consumers. The platform combines real-time vehicle data with historical maintenance records and performance statistics to provide users with a complete overview of a vehicle's condition. By presenting this information through a clear and intuitive interface, AutoInsight enables users to make more informed decisions regarding purchasing, maintenance, and vehicle management.

For car dealerships, the platform provides reliable data that can be used to support sales conversations and increase customer trust. Maintenance companies can use predictive insights to recommend servicing before problems occur, improving both efficiency and customer satisfaction. Vehicle manufacturers benefit from large amounts of collected data that can help identify recurring issues and improve future vehicle designs. Consumers gain greater transparency by accessing detailed information about a vehicle's history, condition, and overall reliability before making purchasing decisions.

During the design process, I focused heavily on creating a clean and intuitive user interface capable of displaying complex datasets without overwhelming the user. Careful attention was given to information hierarchy, typography, color coding, spacing, and navigation to ensure that important information could be understood quickly. I designed multiple wireframes, explored different layout solutions, and created high-fidelity prototypes in Adobe XD to visualize the complete user experience across various devices.

This project significantly strengthened my skills in UX research, responsive interface design, user-centered design principles, and information architecture. It also taught me the importance of designing for different user groups with varying needs while ensuring consistency across platforms. Most importantly, I learned how to translate large amounts of technical data into a visually appealing, accessible, and easy-to-use digital product that balances functionality with an engaging user experience.
